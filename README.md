# Data-Professional-Insights-Analyzing-Roles-Opinions-and-Industry-Trends
A Power BI focus project

## Overview 
The objective of this project was to analyze professionals working in the data field and uncover insights into their backgrounds, roles, compensation, and perspectives within the industry. Using survey-based data collected from data professionals across multiple countries, the analysis focused on understanding trends in job roles, salary satisfaction, career entry difficulty, work-life balance, and overall industry sentiment.

---

## Objectives
The project aimed to break down the demographic and professional composition of data professionals, analyze opinions and sentiment related to compensation and job satisfaction, compare roles, experience levels, and geographic distribution, identify trends in career entry difficulty and work-life balance, and develop an interactive dashboard that effectively communicates key insights.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Data Source** - Survey data collected from professionals working in data-related roles, including information on job titles, salaries, experience levels, geographic location, and professional opinions.
- **Power Query (Power BI)** -Power Query Editor was used extensively for data cleaning and preparation.
  - Removed redundant and ambiguous fields
  - Standardized categorical values
  - Handled missing/inconsistent responses
  - Simplified complex survey answers
  - Consolidated multiple columns to better fit the analytical model and overall project vision.
- **Power BI** -Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize trends and insights derived from the survey data.
   - KPI cards were used to highlight key metrics such as average salary and overall satisfaction levels
   - Bar and column charts compared job roles, salary ranges, and perceived career difficulty
   - Geographic visuals illustrated the distribution of survey respondents by country
   - Additional visuals explored work-life balance, salary satisfaction, and professional sentiment, with slicers and filters enabling dynamic, user-driven analysis

---

## Key Insights
- A substantial portion of respondents indicated that breaking into the data field requires moderate to high effort, highlighting barriers to entry
- Data Scientists consistently appear in the highest salary ranges among surveyed roles
